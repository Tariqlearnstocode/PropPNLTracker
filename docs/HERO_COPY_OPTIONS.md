# Hero Copy Options

Research-backed hero copy directions for Prop PNL. Based on competitive analysis, prop trader forum language, and marketing psychology.

---

## Research Summary

### Audience insights
- Traders spend **$4,270 on average** before their first real payout (PipFarm survey). 60% never reach profitability.
- The single most resonant question: **"Am I actually profitable after all my fees?"**
- Common language: "messy spreadsheet," "stop guessing," "juggling accounts," "hidden fees," "treat trading like a business"
- Traders avoid doing the full math because they **fear the answer**

### Competitive gap
- **No competitor does bank-connected P&L tracking.** Every alternative (Prop Firm Tracker, PropFirmOne, TradesViz, Trading Plan Pro) requires manual entry or broker API.
- Prop Firm Tracker ($5/mo) is the closest — manual entry dashboard with net profit, ROI per firm, PDF tax reports. Their headline: "Stop juggling spreadsheets."
- The bank connection is a genuine, defensible differentiator.

### Psychology at play
- **Loss aversion**: Traders fear discovering they're not profitable. But that same fear, once activated, compels them to find out.
- **Ostrich effect**: Avoiding bad financial news. The copy needs to make avoidance feel more costly than knowing.
- **Zeigarnik effect**: Open questions ("Do you know your real number?") create tension that can only be resolved by using the product.
- **Authority bias**: "Bank-verified" positions the bank — not the trader, not a spreadsheet — as the source of truth.

## Direction 1: "The Question" ⭐ Recommended

Leads with the question every trader asks privately. High emotional activation.

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| **Headline** | Are you actually profitable? **Your bank knows.** |
| **Subline** | Connect your bank. We pull every payout and fee from Topstep, FTMO, Apex, Rise and more — and show your real prop firm P&L. No spreadsheet. No guessing. |
| **CTA** | Get Your Real P&L — Free → |
| **Secondary CTA** | View Plans |
| **Trust bar** | No card required · Cancel anytime |
| **Check marks** | Teller · Read-only · Encrypted · Topstep, FTMO, Rise, Wise |

**Why**: Asks the question they're already asking themselves. "Your bank knows" is a two-word differentiator. Creates an open loop they need to close.

## Direction 2: "The Math You're Avoiding"

Names specific costs. Creates urgency through specificity.

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| **Headline** | Resets, fees, and challenges **add up. Do you know to what?** |
| **Subline** | Connect your bank once. We categorize every prop firm payout and fee automatically — Topstep, FTMO, Rise, Apex and more. Your true P&L in ~60 seconds. |
| **CTA** | See Your Real Numbers — Free → |

**Why**: Names the costs traders know are eroding profits. "Do you know to what?" is an open loop.

## Direction 3: "The Lie"

Vivid conflict between perceived and real P&L.

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| **Headline** | Your spreadsheet says you're profitable. **Your bank account disagrees.** |
| **Subline** | We connect to your bank and track real payouts and fees from Topstep, FTMO, Apex, Rise and more. No manual entry. No missing costs. Just your true prop firm P&L. |
| **CTA** | Connect Your Bank — Free → |

**Why**: Creates conflict between belief (spreadsheet) and reality (bank). Echoes the "Spreadsheets lie" section lower on the page.

## Direction 4: "Current Headline + Stat Sub"

Keeps existing H1 (good SEO), replaces subline with a devastating stat.

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| **Headline** | Prop firm P&L — **bank-verified.** |
| **Subline** | Traders spend $4,270 on average before their first real payout. Connect your bank — we track every payout and fee from Topstep, FTMO, Rise and more so you know exactly where you stand. |
| **CTA** | Get Your Real P&L — Free → |

**Why**: Lowest-risk option. The $4,270 stat does the emotional work. Good if you want to keep SEO positioning intact.

## Direction 5: "60 Seconds of Truth"

Leads with outcome + effort framing. Resolves "is this hard?" immediately.

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| **Headline** | Your real prop firm P&L. **60 seconds. One bank connection.** |
| **Subline** | We pull payouts and fees from Topstep, FTMO, Apex, Rise and more — straight from your bank. No spreadsheet. No manual entry. Just the number that actually matters. |
| **CTA** | Connect Your Bank — Free → |

**Why**: Present bias — people want results now. "The number that actually matters" implies they've been looking at the wrong numbers.

## SEO notes

All directions keep "prop firm P&L" in the headline or subline. Metadata (title, description, OG) should remain keyword-targeted regardless of which hero direction is chosen. The H1 can be more emotional as long as the `<title>` tag stays SEO-optimized.
