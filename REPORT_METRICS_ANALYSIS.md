## 💡 UI/UX IMPROVEMENT RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. **Consolidate Duplicate Metrics**
   - Remove duplicate "Total Purchases" and "Total Payouts" from Activity tab (already in Overview)
   - Remove duplicate "Win Rate" from Performance tab (already in Overview)
   - Remove duplicate "ROI" from Overview (keep in Efficiency where it has more context)
   - Consider showing "Top Firm" only in header, remove from Performance/Efficiency

### 2. **Reorganize Tab Structure**
   - **Overview**: High-level summary + charts (remove detailed metrics)
   - **Monthly**: Detailed monthly breakdown (unique)
   - **Firms**: Firm-by-firm analysis (unique)
   - **Transactions**: Individual transaction management (unique)
   - **Performance**: Time-based patterns (daily calendar, streaks, day-of-week) (unique)
   - **Activity**: Purchase/payout frequency and patterns (unique)
   - **Efficiency**: ROI, margins, growth trends, firm comparisons (unique)

### 3. **Create Shared Metrics Section**
   - Move truly shared metrics (Total Purchases, Total Payouts, Win Rate) to header or a dedicated summary section
   - Avoid repeating in individual tabs

### 4. **Tab-Specific Focus**
   - Each tab should focus on its unique value proposition
   - Remove redundant metrics that don't add context to that specific view

### 5. **Consider Tab Merging**
   - **Performance + Activity**: Could potentially merge since both focus on patterns
   - **Overview + Monthly**: Overview could be a summary view, Monthly could be the detailed view
