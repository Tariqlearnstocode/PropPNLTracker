## Best combination (current — SEO + sell)

The previous "Your real P&L. One click." was too generic: no "prop firm" (zero SEO), and it didn't sell the job or the outcome. The hero below is built for **conversion** (pain → outcome, specificity) and **SEO** (target terms in H1 and sub).

| Element | Pick | Why |
|--------|------|-----|
| **Main** | **Prop firm P&L—** (line 1) + **bank-verified.** (line 2, green) | H1 contains "prop firm P&L" for SEO; "bank-verified" differentiates and builds trust. |
| **Sub** | **Connect your bank. We track payouts and fees from Topstep, FTMO, The5ers, Rise and more—your true profit and loss in ~60 seconds.** | Action + firm names (SEO + credibility) + outcome (true P&L) + time (~60 sec). Second line: "No spreadsheets. No manual entry. One connection." reinforces contrast. |
| **Check marks** | **Teller · Read-only · Encrypted · Topstep, FTMO, Rise, Wise** | Trust (Teller, read-only, encrypted) + supported firms (SEO + "we work with your stack"). |

**Page SEO (landing):** The landing page now has its own `metadata`: title and description include "prop firm P&L", "bank-verified", "Topstep, FTMO, Rise", "payouts and fees", "profit and loss" so search results and shares show targeted copy.

## Notes

- **Main:** Short and benefit-led works best; “Bank-verified” / “real P&L” / “one click” support clarity and time-to-value.
- **Sub:** Keep to one sentence when possible; include outcome (real P&L) and effort (~60 sec) or “no manual entry.”
- **Check marks:** 3–4 items max to avoid clutter. Balance security (Teller, read-only, encrypted) with friction reduction (no card, cancel anytime) depending on what you want to emphasize.
